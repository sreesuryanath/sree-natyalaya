About Us

Sree Natyalaya School of Fine Arts is a premier institution dedicated to nurturing talent and promoting the rich traditions of performing and visual arts. Founded by Smt. S. Preetha, the institution blends classical foundation with contemporary teaching to provide a holistic learning experience.

Welcoming students from the age of 3 and above, Sree Natyalaya fosters creativity, discipline, and artistic growth across all age groups. The highly skilled instructors, structured curricula, and opportunities for stage performance ensure a lifelong appreciation for the fine arts.
